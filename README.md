Privacy Policy – Mogify

Last Updated: October 26, 2025
Developer: Appterra

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Mogify mobile application.

1. Information We Collect
1.1. Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.

1.2. Photos

Photos you upload are stored locally on your device only.
When you submit a photo for analysis or enhancement, it is temporarily sent to Google Gemini for processing.
We do not store your photos on our servers, and they are not retained by our third-party AI provider.

1.3. Usage Data

We may collect information such as features you access, token usage, and in-app interactions to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Provide and maintain the Mogify service

Process photos using AI analysis and enhancement

Manage your account and token balance

Improve app functionality and user experience

Communicate with you about updates or issues

3. Data Storage and Security

Photos remain on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

Account information is stored securely using industry-standard practices.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

4. Third-Party Services
4.1. Google Sign-In

Used for authentication. Your use of Google Sign-In is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy.

4.2. Google Gemini AI

Photos submitted for analysis are temporarily processed by Google Gemini.
They are not stored, not shared, and not used for AI training.

4.3. Google Play

In-app purchases are handled through Google Play, following Google’s Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information as long as your account is active.

If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted from our systems.

Photos stored on your device remain under your control.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access your personal information

Correct inaccurate information

Delete your account and associated data

Opt out of certain data collection practices

7. Children’s Privacy

Mogify is intended for users 13+.
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
If such data is discovered, it will be deleted immediately.

8. International Users

Mogify is operated from Türkiye.
If you use the app from outside Türkiye, your information may be transferred and processed under Turkish data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time.
Changes will be posted within the app.
Continued use of Mogify indicates acceptance of updated terms.
